Services

We provide a variety of services, including:

Developmental Support Around:

  • Communication
  • Emotional Regulation
  • Fine Motor Skills
  • Play Skills
  • Learning Challenges
  • Self Care Skills
  • Effective Social Engagement

Parenting supports around understanding and supporting children who have:

  • Developmental Delay
  • Autism
  • Other Disabilities
  • Or if you are concerned about something undefined and you're looking for backup.

Including Education Supports:

  • Maximising Meaningful Participation
  • Positive Education Journeys

Fees & Expenses

We utilise NDIS funding for both Self-Managed and Plan-Managed participants.


For appointments between Pakenham and Warragul:

  • $193.99, plus $16.16 travel, which includes a 45-minute face to face appointment and 15 minutes preparation time.
  • Preparations of Letters or Reports are charged at $193.99 per hour.
  • Conversations with other providers are also charged at $193.99 per hour, as necessary.

Appointments beyond Pakenham and Warragul will attract an addition $16.16 charge for each additional 15 minutes of travel time.

Cancellations within 24 hours of schedules appointment, or "no shows" will incurr the above hourly fee.

For appointments that are being self-funded by families, please email to discuss.
